About

Hi, I’m Daniel Ting. Welcome to Super Pork Chop, my blog!

As a senior worker bee in a distant land of fire and ice, I teach young roombas the errors of their ways, so they may one day be released into the wild. Half of the time, I’m like one of those horrible deep sea creatures that stay after 5:30 and dwell in the depths of Engineering, debugging nameless things. The rest of the time I float around in the clouds, sending email through the atmosphere and holding my nose while writing bash scripts.

When I’m not playing the flute, doing creative writing, or playing video games, I enjoy programming in C/C++, ARM assembly, python, Forth, and elisp.

My interests lie in computer security, systems programming, and emacs, and I’m frequently attracted to the new and shiny.

These posts are a rambling collection of things that I’ve learned, and I hope you’ll learn something new as well.
